## 📌 What Is This Repository?

This is a centralized open-source repository for comprehensive policy research documents focused on solving systemic, large-scale challenges in India. These documents are not just academic papers—they provide concrete solutions, international benchmarks, budget estimates, and actionable roadmaps for policymakers, researchers, and civil society.

Currently, this repository hosts two major research projects:
1. **Resource Scarcity in India** 
2. **Reforming India's Electoral Roll System (Voter Fraud Reform)**

---

## 📑 Project 1: Resource Scarcity in India

### A Study of Shortages Induced by Pandemic, War, and Climate Change

This project analyses why India repeatedly faces shortages of essential resources — cooking gas, fuel, food, water, medicines, and raw materials — and proposes concrete solutions backed by data, international best practices, and budget estimates.

**Key Highlights:**
- 🔍 **100+ case studies** — from India's Partition (1947) to the Strait of Hormuz crisis (2026)
- 🌍 **8 country comparisons** — Japan, Singapore, Israel, Netherlands, EU, China, South Korea/Taiwan, USA
- 📋 **23 specific policy recommendations** with implementation timelines
- 💰 **₹2,29,475 crore budget** broken down by phase and ministry
- 📊 **18 measurable KPIs** for tracking progress
- 📜 **3 draft laws** ready for policy adoption (NSCEA, Energy Security Act, National Water Authority)
- 🗺️ **State-level vulnerability mapping** for 10 most at-risk states
- 📉 **Cost of Inaction analysis** — India loses ₹3,00,000 crore/year by NOT acting (6.5x ROI on investment)

## 🗳️ Project 2: Reforming India's Electoral Roll System

### An AI-Driven Framework for Eliminating Voter Fraud

This project proposes a comprehensive, socio-technical reform framework to secure India's electoral roll—the foundation of the world's largest democracy—against fraudulent additions, systematic deletions, and impersonation.

**Key Highlights:**
- 🏛️ **5-Pillar Reform Framework** — Automatic enrollment via civil registry, AI spam detection, tamper-proof public audit logs, biometric authentication, and an independent ombudsman.
- 🌍 **International Benchmarks** — Policy analyses from Germany, Australia, and Estonia.
- 💻 **AI Anomaly Detection Models** — Technical architectures for identifying bulk registration anomalies, duplicate photos (using FaceNet), and systemic deletions (using Time-Series Forecasting).
- 💰 **Budget Estimation** — Detailed ₹4,400 Crore 5-year budget model.
- 🛡️ **Residual Risk Assessment** — Transparent analysis of vulnerabilities like civil registry gaps and AI model biases.
